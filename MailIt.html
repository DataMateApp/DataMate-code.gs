Mail It

Instructions:
1. Select the sheet containing the data to include in the email body.
2. Enter the range (e.g., A1:G48) to copy into the email.
3. Choose "All Contacts" or "Specific Contacts" to select recipients.
4. If selecting specific contacts, hold Ctrl (or Cmd) to select multiple emails.
5. Enter the email subject.
6. Click "Submit" to send emails.
